The Philadelphia Story is the story of a journalist who is sent to do a newspaper story about a socialite couple who are getting married. He brings his photographer/girlfriend with him, though their relationship is rocky. When they arrive, the journalists begin flirting with the engaged couple, and after some comedy-of-errors encounters, both original couples leave committed to their relationships.

Macaulay Connor is a journalist who wants to write significant stories who has been assigned to write a social article about rich people getting married. He is played by Jimmy Stewart.

Tracy Lord is a strong, wealthy socialite who feels like everyone just expects her to get married, but wants to actually DO something with her life. She is played by Katherine Hepburn.
